ABOUT US

Tipsy Panda was established in 2017 in Virar(Northren Mumbai Suburb)and since then has made a name in the local food and beverage scene.Known for its varied offerings of indo-chinese inspired dishes. Tipsy Panda is a go to name in almost all households when it comes to Taste,Hygiene,Consistency,Customer service and Affordability.Six years and two Restaurents later the brand Tipsy Panda is ready to expand its wings with the launch of our cloud kitchen franchise model "Tipsy Panda Express".
